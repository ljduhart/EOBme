Privacy Policy for EOBme

Effective Date: April 29, 2026

EOBme LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), founded by Lester Duhart, operates the EOBme mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Camera Permission

The app requires access to your device's camera to allow you to scan and upload your Explanation of Benefits (EOB) documents. Images captured by the camera are processed locally on your device to extract relevant billing information.

Personal and Financial Data

While using our Service, the app extracts data from your EOBs, such as provider names, deductible amounts, copays, and coinsurance. This data is used solely to provide you with a breakdown of your patient responsibility and to generate appeal letters.

Biometric Data

The app may use biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint or face recognition) provided by your device's operating system to secure access to the app. We do not have access to your actual biometric data; we only receive a confirmation of successful authentication from the system.

2. On-Device Processing and Storage

All sensitive data, including scanned images and extracted billing information, is processed and stored locally on your device.

  • OCR Processing: We use on-device machine learning (Google ML Kit) to recognize text. Your images are not sent to any external servers for processing.
  • Local Database: Extracted information and generated appeal letters are stored in a local database (Room) on your device.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, or disclose your personal or medical information to any third parties. Since your data is stored locally, we do not have access to it.

4. Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We utilize your device's built-in security features (like biometric locks) to help protect your data.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
