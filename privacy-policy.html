Privacy Policy for EOBme

Effective Date: June 9, 2026

EOBme LLC ("we," "us," or "our"), founded by LJ Duhart, operates the EOBme mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, processing, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect several different types of information for various purposes to provide and improve our Service to you.

Camera Permission and Document Scanning

The app requires access to your device's camera or photo library to allow you to scan and upload your Explanation of Benefits (EOB) documents. We use this capability solely to process and analyze your insurance billing documents.

Personal and Financial Data

While using our Service, the app extracts data from your uploaded EOBs, such as provider names, deductible amounts, copays, coinsurance, and service dates. This data is used to provide you with a breakdown of your patient responsibility, track your out-of-pocket maximums, and generate appeal letters.

Biometric Data

The app may use biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint or face recognition) provided by your device's operating system to secure access to the app. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your actual biometric data; we only receive a confirmation of successful authentication from your device.

2. Data Processing and Cloud Storage

To provide advanced document analysis and secure cross-device syncing, we utilize industry-leading, highly secure third-party services:

  • Veryfi: When you upload an EOB document, the image is securely transmitted to Veryfi, a SOC2 Type 2 compliant service, for Optical Character Recognition (OCR) and data extraction. Your documents and extracted data are managed within Veryfi's secure vault architecture.
  • Google Firebase: Your user profile, extracted billing information, and app preferences are stored in Google Firebase (Firestore). We use Firebase Authentication to securely manage your login credentials. This allows you to securely access your EOBme data across multiple devices.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or medical information to any third parties.

Your data is only shared with the trusted infrastructure partners mentioned above (Veryfi and Google Firebase) strictly for the purpose of operating the Service, processing your documents, and storing your data securely. These providers are bound by strict data protection agreements.

4. Security

The security of your data is paramount to us. We utilize secure, encrypted connections (HTTPS) when transmitting your data to our infrastructure partners (Veryfi and Firebase). While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

5. User Data Rights and Account Deletion

You have the right to access, update, or delete the information we have on you. You can manage your profile information directly within the EOBme app's Settings menu.

Account Deletion: You may request the complete deletion of your account and all associated data (including all EOB records stored in Firebase and Veryfi) at any time. You can initiate this process directly through the "Delete Account" option within the app's Settings menu, or by contacting us directly at the email provided below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
